Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Roll out the dough onto a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden.

Classic Burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Form beef into patties.
  2. Cook patties on grill for 4–5 minutes per side.
  3. Toast buns lightly.
  4. Add cheese to patties.
  5. Assemble burger with toppings.

Chocolate Sundae

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Sprinkles
  • Cherry

Instructions

  1. Scoop ice cream into a bowl.
  2. Drizzle chocolate syrup on top.
  3. Add whipped cream.
  4. Sprinkle decorations.
  5. Top with a cherry and serve.
